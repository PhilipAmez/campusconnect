Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is PeerLoom?

PeerLoom is a social learning app designed like WhatsApp but built for students. It allows you to form study groups, chat, and use AI to enhance your studies.

What is PeerPal?

PeerPal is our built-in AI assistant. It can explain topics, help summarize notes, and provide real-time academic support directly in your group chats.

Do I need to pay to use PeerLoom?

Nope! PeerLoom is completely free for students.

Do I have to select my school before using PeerLoom?

No. PeerLoom is open to all students. You can join or create groups without selecting a specific school.

How is PeerLoom different from WhatsApp or Telegram?

Unlike general messaging apps, PeerLoom is built specifically for students. Study groups, AI integration, and academic-focused features make it a one-of-a-kind learning hub.

Can I invite my friends?

Yes! You can add friends easily and create your own study groups with them inside PeerLoom.
